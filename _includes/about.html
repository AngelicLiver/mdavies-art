About Me

I am a Veterinary Surgeon in South East England, art is both my hobby and a creative outlet. I have always loved animals and keep several pet rats along with two dogs. I am sure that my family of animals will continue to grow.

Passionate about art I have been painting from a young age and have sold my art to private collectors since 2006. As well as one-off pieces I also sell a variety of prints and merchandise of my own design.

Animals are my preferred subject of choice but I try to keep my works diverse with; life, landscapes and fantasy. I also work in many other mediums such as inks, markers, collage, digital etc.

My clients and peers commend the empathy that I am told my works exude, particularly my pet-portraits. I am deeply moved to hear stories of provided relief, however small, for those experiencing bereavement for their pets.

Mousalopes Exist! is my webcomic - a comic about some cryptozoological creatures I created and their (mis)adventures. This is where the character 'Pannya' comes from.
